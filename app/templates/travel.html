Getting there is easy. The Googleplex, 1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy, Mountain View, California 94043

From the San Francisco (SFO) Airport
By Car (23 miles, 26 minutes) Take 101 South for about 20 miles to the Rengstorff Exit. Exit Rengstorff, keep right at the fork, and follow signs for Amphitheatre Parkway. Merge onto Rengstorff Ave, then continue onto Amphitheater Pkwy. Google will be on the right at 1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy.



From the San Jose (SJC) Airport
By Car (12.3 miles, 15 minutes) Take Skyport Drive until you merge onto CA-87 North toward US-101 North. Take exit 9B on the left to merge onto US-101 North toward San Francisco. Travel about 9 miles. Take exit 400A toward Amphitheater Parkway. Merge onto Rengstorff. Continue onto Amphitheatre Pkwy. Google will be on the right at 1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy.
